Help Button

The Help Button allows a person using our app to send an alert to a carer where they can then choose between calling their carer or talking to an AI-Chatbot if they are not available.

When the help button is used it will send a notification to the carer letting them now it has been used while the patient can send additional information to there carer like the severity of their issue and then they can write a piece of text to the carer for additional information on what their issue might be if they choose to.
